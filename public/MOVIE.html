Nuel's Movie Ranking

Top 4 Movies

Snowfall

A crime drama set in 1980s Los Angeles that follows the rise of the crack cocaine epidemic, showing how it impacts dealers, addicts, and even the CIA. The story mainly centers on Franklin Saint, a young hustler trying to build an empire while navigating power, family, and survival.

Breaking Bad

A legendary TV series about Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher who turns to cooking meth after being diagnosed with cancer. What starts as a desperate move to provide for his family spirals into a dark journey of power, greed, and moral collapse, showing how an ordinary man transforms into the feared drug kingpin “Heisenberg.”

Prison Break

A thriller series about Michael Scofield, a genius engineer who deliberately gets himself locked up to help his wrongly accused brother, Lincoln Burrows, escape from death row. What starts as one big prison escape turns into a rollercoaster of conspiracies, government cover-ups, and survival games across multiple prisons and countries.

Dexter

A crime drama about Dexter Morgan, a blood-spatter analyst for the Miami police who secretly lives as a vigilante serial killer. By day he helps solve murders, and by night he hunts down criminals who slipped through the justice system all while struggling to balance his dark urges with his personal life and relationships.
